AR. GAGAN MAHANA

Founder, Principal Designer | Arch Studio 18

Gagan had planned to start his firm in 2018 but eventually it materialized in 2019. His family business being related construction and construction materials, his firm was just the right combination for the already existing business.

A Journey Driven by Passion

Though Arch Studio18's inception was originally planned for 2018, Gagan felt compelled to pursue a deeper understanding of design by immersing himself in the world of interior architecture after completing his schooling. However, a year later, his unwavering attraction to architecture led him to embrace a fresh start and embark on a quest to master the fundamentals.

Academic Excellence and Professional Growth

Completing his Bachelor's in Architecture from the prestigious institution in Chandigarh, Gagan wasted no time in gaining hands-on experience. He joined a residential-focused firm as a novice, despite facing challenges such as inadequate compensation and delayed payments. Undeterred by these hurdles, Gagan maintained a positive outlook, recognizing the importance of mastering the intricacies of both office and on-site work.

Seizing Opportunities

Through dedication and determination, Gagan rapidly honed his skills, leveraging the invaluable experience gained during his tenure at the firm. As the year progressed, he developed the confidence and expertise required to establish his own architectural and interior design consultancy service. Leveraging its own invested builder projects and extensive network within the materials industry, Arch Studio18 quickly gained recognition for its exemplary services. They have been featured in magazines like The Architect's Diary.com, Architects and Interiors India, Design Essentia Magazine and Architects & Builder's Magazine.

Embracing Innovation

Arch Studio18 exemplifies a firm that seamlessly blends innovation and tradition. By drawing upon the deep-rooted family business connections, the company possesses a unique understanding of materials, construction, and design. This invaluable expertise enables Arch Studio18 to offer comprehensive and forwardthinking solutions, tailored to meet the needs of their diverse clientele.
